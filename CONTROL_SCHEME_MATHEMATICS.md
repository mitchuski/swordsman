## The Numbers: 8 and 7

### Why 8 Spells (Mage)

```
8 = 6 orbit + 2 reserve

    ✦ Orbit (active)     ✦ Reserve (swappable)
    ────────────────     ────────────────────
    🛡️ Shield            💎 Crystallize
    👁️ Insight           📜 Proverb
    🔒 Seal
    ✨ Clarity
    🔥 Purge
    🌙 Shadow
```

**8** represents:
- The **octave** — completion of a cycle, return to origin at higher level
- **I Ching trigrams** — 8 fundamental states (☰☱☲☳☴☵☶☷)
- **Directions** — cardinal + ordinal = 8 points of orientation
- **Bits in a byte** — the fundamental unit of digital information

The Mage deals in **information** and **understanding**. 8 spells = 8 bits = one byte of sovereign data.

### Why 7 Swords (Swordsman)

```
7 = 6 active + 1 reserve

    ⚔ Active (stances)   ⚔ Reserve (blade)
    ──────────────────   ─────────────────
    🛡️ Guardian          📜 Proverb
    ✂️ Minimalist
    🏰 Fortress
    👑 Sovereign
    🌑 Shadow
    👁️ Witness
```

**7** represents:
- The **chakras** — 7 energy centers, sovereignty over the body
- **Days of creation** — 6 days work + 1 day rest
- **Musical notes** — 7 tones before the octave returns
- **The 7th capital** — privacy as the foundational capital

The Swordsman deals in **assertion** and **posture**. 7 swords = 7 stances = complete coverage of the sovereignty spectrum.

## The Ratio: 8:7

```
Mage : Swordsman = 8 : 7

    8/7 ≈ 1.142857142857...

    The repeating decimal 142857 is the "cyclic number"
    — multiply by any digit 1-6 and get the same digits rotated
```

This ratio creates **asymmetric harmony**:
- Not equal (that would be static)
- Not vastly different (that would be imbalanced)
- The slight excess of Mage spells reflects the emissary's **reactive** nature — more options for quick response
- The Swordsman's 7 reflects the master's **deliberate** nature — completeness without excess

### The Crossing

Interestingly, the left brain controls the **right hand** and vice versa. This creates a meaningful cross:

```
LEFT BRAIN ──────╲╱────── RIGHT HAND (mouse)
                 ╱╲
RIGHT BRAIN ────╱  ╲───── LEFT HAND (less used)

But on the mouse:
- Left Click (index finger) → Mage (left brain: sequential)
- Right Click (middle finger) → Swordsman (right brain: spatial)
```

The **physical action** (clicking) crosses to match the **cognitive mode**:
- Quick tap (left click) = sequential action = left brain = Mage
- Hold and release (right click) = spatial deliberation = right brain = Swordsman

## The Reserve System

### Why Reserves Exist

```
ACTIVE                              RESERVE
══════                              ═══════
Immediately accessible              Swappable in editor
In the radial menu                  Stored for later
Quick selection                     Intentional configuration

Mage:   6 orbit  +  2 reserve  =  8 total
Sword:  6 active +  1 reserve  =  7 total
```

**Reserves enable personalization** without overwhelming the radial menu:
- Keep 6 items in quick-access (optimal for 60° segments)
- Store alternatives in reserve
- Swap via editor (`M` or `S` keys)

### Reserve Ratios

```
Mage:   2/8 = 25% in reserve
Sword:  1/7 ≈ 14% in reserve

The Mage has more variety (emissary adapts)
The Swordsman has more constancy (master maintains)
```
