# Swordsman Control Scheme

## The Master's Way: Right Side = Right Click

The Swordsman is the **Master**—deliberate, spatial, holistic. The right brain.

### Controls

| Action | Input | Behavior |
|--------|-------|----------|
| **View Stances** | Right-click hold | Radial menu appears showing available stances |
| **Choose Stance** | Move mouse while holding | Highlight moves to stance under cursor |
| **Cast Stance** | Release right-click | Selected stance is cast at cursor position |
| **Quick Cast** | Quick right-click tap | Immediately casts current stance |
| **Stance Editor** | `S` key | Opens full stance configuration editor |

### Available Stances

| Stance | Emoji | MyTerms Mapping | Description |
|--------|-------|-----------------|-------------|
| Guardian | 🛡️ | `DO_NOT_TRACK` | Block tracking attempts |
| Minimalist | ✂️ | `DATA_MINIMISATION` | Minimal data only |
| Fortress | 🏰 | `COOKIE_ESSENTIAL_ONLY` | Essential cookies only |
| Sovereign | 👑 | `FULL_SOVEREIGNTY` | Assert full control |
| Shadow | 🌑 | `DO_NOT_SELL` | Maximum privacy |
| Witness | 👁️ | `READ_ONLY` | Observe only |

### Philosophy

> *"The master deliberates. You hold, choose, release. The stance is the posture you maintain—a statement of sovereignty that persists."*

The right brain processes:
- Holistic patterns
- Spatial relationships
- The overall posture/stance

This maps to the Swordsman's role: maintaining a **stance** (a persistent privacy posture) that guards your sovereignty across the page.
